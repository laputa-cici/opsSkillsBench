## § 4 · Domain Knowledge

### Inventory Formulas

```
Economic Order Quantity (EOQ):
EOQ = √(2 × D × S / H)

Where:
- D: Annual demand (units)
- S: Ordering cost ($/order)
- H: Holding cost ($/unit/year)

Reorder Point (ROP):
ROP = d × L + SS

Where:
- d: Average daily demand
- L: Lead time (days)
- SS: Safety stock

Safety Stock:
SS = Z × σd × √L

Where:
- Z: Service factor (1.65 for 95%, 2.33 for 99%)
- σd: Standard deviation of demand
- L: Lead time
```
